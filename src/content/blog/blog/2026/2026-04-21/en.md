On April 21, 2026, my third blog went online. I've finally started writing again. This time, it is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. It’s my first time using Cloudflare to host both my domain and website, and it feels quite fast and simple.

## My Previous Blogging Experiences

The first time I wanted to start a personal blog was at the end of 2024, when I had just entered my second year of high school. At that time, I felt that Moments and various social media platforms were too crowded, and I wanted a small space of my own. So I used MkDocs Material to build my first blog. Following the official documentation step by step, my first blog was launched on GitHub Pages. I documented the entire deployment process and the issues I encountered in an article and published it there. Unfortunately, due to various research commitments and extracurricular activities, I was extremely busy. At the same time, participating in the HiMCM competition left me with no motivation to update. After just a few weeks, my first blog repository was deleted.

The second time, I saw a particularly beautiful theme on Bilibili, which sparked my interest again. At that time, I had made a bit of money from CS item trading, and I was also interested in learning about full-stack blog deployment and maintenance. So I bought a server on Rainyun, and my second blog was launched. During that period, it was the time of major exams, and there were many recordings and school concert performances, which provided plenty of photos and videos to update. However, I once again stopped updating later on, and the server was eventually lost due to not renewing the subscription.

## Setting Sail for the Third Time

Life is always unpredictable, and plans often fail to keep up with change. While being busy with life, I was also forced to put down the act of recording life itself.

In 2026, this is already the third time I’ve had the idea of starting a blog. The previous two attempts didn't last, either because of academic pressure or simply due to lack of persistence. This third attempt begins at the time of my high school graduation, just before heading to study in the United States. Writing these words now should serve as motivation for me to keep going this time.
